How to Play

1. Hover over squares on the map to find the # of the square for your adventure.

2. During the 24-hour snapshot window, send one Blue Team Flag with the memo containing the square # to wax id: SummerShiloh (Tutorial GIF)

3. Wait for the adventures to begin.

4. Your crushies will continue to adventure in the same square unless you send another flag during the next 24-hour snapshot window.

Note: The higher the difficulty, the better the reward chance for rarer NFTs. Blue Team Flags are discarded after each use.
